Workout Plans

Strength Training

An overview of our 5-day split workouts for strength training looks like this:

Day 1: Upper Body – Strength

Day 2: Lower Body – Strength

Day 3: Chest, Shoulders, and Triceps – Hypertrophy

Day 4: Back and Biceps – Hypertrophy

Day 5: Legs and Calves – Hypertrophy

Here is an outline of a week of training.

Workout 1, Upper Body (Strength)

  • Bench Press
  • Overhead Press
  • Barbell Row
  • Bar Dip
  • Hammer Curl

Workout 2, Lower Body (Strength)

  • Squat
  • Deadlift
  • Leg Curl
  • Calf Raises

Workout 3, Chest, Shoulders, and Triceps (Hypertrophy)

  • Bench Press
  • Incline Dumbbell Press
  • Standing Cable Chest Fly or Dumbbell Flyes
  • Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press
  • Dumbell Lateral Raise
  • Barbell Lying Triceps Extension
  • Overhead Cable Triceps Extension

Workout 4, Backs and Biceps (Hypertrophy)

  • Lat Pulldown
  • Seated Cable Row
  • Dumbbell Row
  • Preacher Curl
  • Barbell Curl

Workout 5, Legs and Calves (Hypertrophy)

  • Squat
  • Leg Press
  • Romanian Deadlifts
  • Leg Extensions
  • Calf Raises

Muscle Building

An overview of our 4-day split workouts for muscle building looks like this:

Day 1: Chest and Triceps - Mass

Day 2: Back and Biceps – Mass

Day 3: Shoulders and abs – Hypertrophy

Day 4: Legs and Calves – Hypertrophy

Here is an outline of a week of training.

Workout 1, Chest and Triceps

  • Bench Press
  • Incline Dumbbell Press
  • Standing Cable Chest Fly
  • Push-up
  • Barbell lying Tricep Extensions
  • Overhead Cable Tricep Extensions
  • Tricep Pushdown

Workout 2, Back and Biceps

  • Barrbell Row
  • Lat Pulldown
  • Seated Cable Row
  • Shrugs
  • Back Extension
  • Barbell Curl
  • Hammer Curl
  • Incline Dumbbell Curl

Workout 3, Shoulders and Abs

  • Dumbbell Should Press
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raise
  • Dumbbell Front Raise
  • Reverse Machine Fly
  • Hanging Knee Raise
  • Cable Crunch
  • Kneeling Ab Wheel Roll-out
  • Oblique Crunch

Workout 4, Legs and Calves

  • Lying Leg Curl
  • Squat
  • Leg Press
  • Bulgarian Split Squat
  • Leg Extension
  • Seated leg curl
  • Romanian Deadlift
